About Us

At éCo pelouse, we are passionate about the environment and determined to make a difference in our community. We believe in the power of innovation and commitment to create a more sustainable and greener future.

Our story began with a simple vision: to offer an environmentally friendly service without compromising on quality. Driven by our love for nature and our desire to preserve our planet for future generations, we founded éCo pelouse with the goal of changing the way lawns are maintained. The name embodies our commitment to the environment. By choosing a spelling that emphasizes the C of carbon, we underline our commitment to reducing carbon emissions associated with lawn maintenance.

As young entrepreneurs, we bring a unique energy and creativity to our business. We are constantly looking for new ideas and innovative solutions to meet the needs of our customers while respecting our commitment to the environment. We use exclusively electric mowers powered by renewable energy sources to ensure carbon-free lawn cutting. We believe that every little bit counts, and we are committed to doing our part to protect our planet.

In short, we are proud of our company and our commitment to sustainability. We look forward to working with you to create beautiful, healthy, and environmentally friendly outdoor spaces. Join us in our mission to make our world a better place, one lawn at a time.
